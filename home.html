Playground Projects

Practice Makes Perfect.

What is this?

Developers should be practicing their craft all the time, this website will offer you with cool and free projects you can build in different technologies, programming languages and tech stacks.

Okay, Why should I use this?

There’s a lot of coding problems websites, but few that offers actual full projects, instead of wasting ton of time searching for projects online, this website will offer you with a huge library of free projects in one place.

Cool, How can I use this?

Click on Projects on the header, chose the technology you like, then scroll to find the project that you want and can build, click on it, it will lead you to a link with the resources you will need to craft the project, build it, and share it with the world on GitHub.
